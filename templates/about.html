About

Cup of Paws is a cafe where we provide extraordinary things that no other cafes provide in NYC. Our cafe is just a regular cafe but with puppies.

    This cafe is directed to animal lovers and college students who likes to spend quality time by themselves with a puppy. We also have options for adoption and we are connected with our local shelters.If you would like to spend time with an animal at our store and decide to adopt it, 40% of the profit goes to the shelter and rest is to us. Cup of Paws is a place where relaxation is taken to another level and people can really enjoy spending their time here.
